ATM GLOBAL BUSINESS SCHOOL (ATM-GBS)

ATM Global Business School (ATM-GBS) was founded in 1999 under aegis of Shree Vaishno Education Society by Ex- IMTians to impart quality education and outstanding learning experience to its students at affordable price. It is promoted by a group of eminent educationists & industrial professionals with a commitment to develop and deliver industry-focused programmes and build a solid foundation for the future of its students. ATM-GLOBAL BUSINESS SCHOOL has a mission to transform the career conscious youth into successfully employed professionals in various fields of specialization with high levels of competencies with rich experience to support them to excel and grow in their life.
