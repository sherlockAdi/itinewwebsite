From Director’s Desk

The dawn of a new technology integrated business has evolved into a career savvy generation. The emerging environment has brought about a marked change in contemporary education system. Apart from sound conceptual grounding in business management there has been a well recognized need to supplement learning to stimulate self-awareness, intuition, cross-cultural sensitivity, entrepreneurial skills with socially responsible and ethical approach. Critical thinking, action-planning, teamwork, creativity as well as individual and organizational development are key differentiations in the modern business education delivery system. ATM-GBS provides students with a rich classroom experience thus generating unparalleled opportunities to expand learning horizon of the real world of business, government administration and functioning of non-profit sector. ATMGBS has excellent infrastructure and continuously strives to create resources that would help generate additional skills that provide cutting edge advantage to its students in realizing their full potential. ATM-GBS offers multiple career enhancing management programs integrating knowledge with practice and focusing on development of individuals. It carries a tradition of excellence to be proud of. A new dawn of opportunities and recognition awaits you post ATM-GBS qualification!

Prof. (Dr.) Shameena Gupta
Director
ATM Global Business School

About Us

ATM Global Business School (ATM-GBS) was founded in 1999 under aegis of Shree Vaishno Education Society by Ex- IMTians to impart quality education and outstanding learning experience to its students at affordable price. It is promoted by a group of eminent educationists & industrial professionals with a commitment to develop and deliver industry-focused programmes and build a solid foundation for the future of its students.
